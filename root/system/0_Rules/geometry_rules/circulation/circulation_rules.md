# Circulation Rules (Geometry)

Horizontal circulation:
- may connect rooms, buildings, or sovereign containers
- may be interior or exterior
- semantics assigns meaning (hallway, road, walkway)

Vertical circulation:
- may connect levels within a container
- may be stairs or elevators
- semantics assigns meaning (staircase, lift, tower ascent)

Circulation does not contain anything.
Circulation does not define behavior.
Circulation does not define semantics.
