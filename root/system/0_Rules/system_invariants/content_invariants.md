# Content Invariants

Content invariants define the physics of content as a downstream, non‑structural, non‑semantic‑driving domain.

Content cannot influence system physics, structure, geometry, naming, or execution.

---

# 1. Content Position Invariant

## 1.1 Content is always downstream
Content is downstream of:
- structure  
- geometry  
- naming  
- registry  
- semantics  
- execution  

Content cannot move upstream.

## 1.2 Content cannot influence physics
Content cannot:
- alter boundaries  
- alter geometry  
- alter categories  
- alter naming rules  

Content is inert with respect to system physics.

---

# 2. Content Interpretation Invariant

## 2.1 Content cannot interpret itself
Content cannot:
- assign meaning  
- infer intent  
- reinterpret structure  

## 2.2 Content cannot be interpreted by upstream domains
Upstream domains cannot:
- derive semantics from content  
- derive structure from content  
- derive identity from content  

Interpretation is a semantic domain, not a content domain.

---

# 3. Content Stability Invariant

## 3.1 Content cannot drift
Content cannot:
- reinterpret itself  
- accumulate meaning  
- shed meaning  
- fuse with semantics  

## 3.2 Content cannot mutate upstream domains
Content cannot:
- change geometry  
- change structure  
- change naming  
- change categories  

Content is passive.

---

# 4. Content Contamination Invariant

## 4.1 Content cannot absorb semantics
Content cannot:
- encode meaning  
- encode identity  
- encode categories  

## 4.2 Content cannot absorb structure
Content cannot:
- define shapes  
- define boundaries  
- define geometry  

Content is not structural.

---

# 5. Content Execution Invariant

## 5.1 Execution cannot reinterpret content
Execution cannot:
- assign meaning  
- infer intent  
- reinterpret content  

Execution is mechanical.

## 5.2 Content cannot drive execution
Content cannot:
- trigger movement  
- alter routing  
- alter engine behavior  

Execution is upstream of content.

---

# 6. Content Domain Separation Invariant

## 6.1 Content cannot fuse with semantics
Semantics ≠ content.

## 6.2 Content cannot fuse with registry
Registry stores structure, not content.

## 6.3 Content cannot fuse with engine
Engine executes; content is inert.

## 6.4 Content cannot fuse with naming
Names cannot encode content.
